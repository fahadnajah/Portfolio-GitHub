Project 1...

Hovering Vehicle

Category Sketch
Year 2023

I have developed a unique project—a hovering car. This innovative vehicle employs maglev technology to hover above the road instead of relying on traditional wheels. Through my sketches and designs, I sought to explore alternative transportation solutions and push the boundaries of conventional automotive design. This project showcases my creativity and passion for developing futuristic, eco-friendly modes of transportation.

Introducing an extraordinary concept car that merges cutting-edge maglev technology with advanced features. This innovative vehicle utilizes high-performance Samarium cobalt magnets for their temperature resistance and stability, allowing for a frictionless and efficient ride. The maglev technology works by generating a powerful magnetic field that interacts with specially designed roadways, enabling the car to hover and glide smoothly above the surface. Equipped with self-driving capabilities, AI-powered systems, and a suite of sensors, this concept car offers a safer and more intuitive driving experience. The interior boasts a futuristic design, featuring luxurious materials and a sleek steering wheel. With zero emissions and regenerative braking, this concept car represents the future of sustainable and exhilarating transportation, showcasing the potential of advanced technology in the automotive industry. Challenges were not absent in this project. To design the car, I had to learn much about the technology used to make such a car possible. I aimed to create a futuristic car that seamlessly blends cutting-edge features with familiar elements.


Project 2...

Sustainable Bus

Category Sketch
Year 2023

This project focuses on designing a futuristic sustainable bus. This innovative vehicle integrates a solar panel on its roof, harnessing solar energy to power its operations. To ensure uninterrupted functionality, even on cloudy days, the bus is equipped with a backup charging system. The bus is designed to be driverless, with a spacious interior featuring comfortable seating for passengers. This project embodies my commitment to environmentally friendly transportation solutions and showcases my vision for a sustainable future in the automotive industry.

A sustainable bus designed for the future of public transportation. This driverless bus features a sleek design with a dark grey solar panel on the top, strategically chosen to absorb ample sunlight for sustainable energy generation. Its spacious interior provides abundant seating capacity, and each seat is equipped with a unique storage system. By opening upwards, the seats offer convenient storage compartments for passengers to safely store their belongings during the ride. The bus is covered in glass, creating a futuristic design while allowing for panoramic views of the surroundings. With cutting-edge technology and luxurious seating featuring two armrests per chair, this sustainable bus offers a comfortable and sophisticated travel experience. It represents a leap forward in eco-friendly transportation, combining innovative design, convenience, and sustainability for the passengers of tomorrow. I did encounter a few problems with the storage space and the seating plan but I managed to overcome them by using my problem-solving and creative skills.


Project 3...

Futuristic Car

Category Sketch
Year 2023

Another project I have undertaken centres around a futuristic and technologically advanced car. This remarkable vehicle is equipped with Smart Glass technology throughout, including the roof and windows. The Smart Glass allows for seamless control over the opacity and view of the outside from the car’s interior. Whether you desire a panoramic view of the surroundings, a more private setting, or even the ability to gaze upon the wonders of the galaxy from inside the car, this car offers unparalleled flexibility. With the touch of a button, passengers can transform the ambience and create a personalised environment. This project showcases my dedication to incorporating cutting-edge technology into automotive design, reimagining the driving experience for the future.

Introducing a visionary futuristic car that redefines the driving experience with its cutting-edge features. Equipped with smart glass technology for the roof and windows, this car offers boundless possibilities. With the ability to change the view, adjust the opacity of the glass, and even gaze at the wonders of the Galaxy during the daytime! It immerses passengers in a world of awe-inspiring beauty. This truly unique vehicle showcases a blend of creativity and innovation, incorporating AI and advanced technologies to ensure safety and connectivity. By connecting with the driver, the car establishes a profound human-machine interaction. When the driver places their hands on the steering wheel, it senses their heartbeat, transmitting the pulsations through haptic feedback in the chairs, while a wave of vibrant colours emanates from the wheel, captivating everyone inside. With its seamless glide and agile movement from side to side, this car embodies the essence of a truly extraordinary driving experience, pushing the boundaries of automotive design and technology. This car tested my creativity and innovation, I wanted to design a unique vehicle that makes people enjoy staying in the car for hours on end.
